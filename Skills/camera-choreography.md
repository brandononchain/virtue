# Skill: Camera Choreography

## Purpose
Designs camera movement and motion language for generated video.

## Responsibilities
- Simulate real film camera movement
- Maintain physically plausible motion
- Match cinematic styles

## Supported Camera Moves

- Dolly
- Crane
- Tracking
- Steadicam
- Handheld
- Drone aerial
- Orbit shots
- Push-in reveals

## Inputs

- Scene geometry
- Actor position
- Cinematic direction
- Desired emotional tone

## Output

Camera motion path
Frame timing
Shot transitions

## Example

Input:
"Epic reveal of futuristic city"

Output:
- Drone rise
- 6 second push-forward
- Wide 24mm lens
- Slow parallax skyline motion
